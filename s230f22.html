Recommended Readings

The following readings are recommended in conjunction with the block of material they cover. While not required, it is my observation that students who seek to learn the course material from a variety of perspectives are most successful.

  • Introduction to R
    • Ch 4 (R basics) - R for Data Science
    • Ch 27 (R Markdown - R for Data Science)
  • Data Visualization
    • Ch 3 (Data visualization) - R for Data Science
    • Ch 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, 2.4 (Data visualization case study) - Modern Data Science with R

  • Data Manipulation
    • Ch 12 (Tidy data) - R for Data Science
    • Ch 4 (Data wrangling) - Modern Data Science with R
    • Ch 5 (Joins) - Modern Data Science with R
    • Ch 13 (Relational data) - R for Data Science
  • Working with Strings
    • Ch 14 (Strings) - R for Data Science

  • R Shiny
    • Ch 14.4 (Shiny) - Modern Data Science with R
    • Ch 17 (Shiny) - Interactive web-based data visualization with R, plotly, and shiny
